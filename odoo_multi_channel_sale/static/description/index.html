Odoo Multi-channel Sale

The perfect solution to your multi-channel marketing strategy. Blend your stores on various multiple channels like Amazon, eBay, Etsy and more under the same roof. Manage various multiple E-commerce channels using a single Odoo database. Integrate and Manage every aspect, from order placement to stock management to delivery operations diligently. Import Products, Products categories, customers, shipping methods etc. to Odoo from each channel. Automatic synchronization of data between Odoo and various channels to help you run the operations without delays.

Data in a glance- One stop solution for your multi-channel marketing strategy

  • Neatly designed Dashboard to overview all the channels integrated with Odoo.

  • Various details of each marketplace/store can be viewed directly from the dashboard.

  • Information about the number of orders placed in the system, Products, product categories and customers can be read separately for each E-commerce channel can be viewed from the dashboard.

  • The Odoo admin can view and manage any detail by clicking on the tab from the dashboard.

  • Sync data from multiple e-commerce channels to Odoo seamlessly.

  • The data can be synchronized between the Odoo database and various e-commerce channels by the click of a button.

  • The Data can also be set to get automatically synced periodically on various channels from Odoo.

  • Moreover, you can upload a CSV file to import/update data to Odoo for sales orders, products, products categories, customers and shipping methods etc.

Say No To Manual Entry

No need to enter data manually in Odoo. Simply Import/Update data using CSV file or data like Sale orders, products, Categories, Customers/Partners, and shipping methods from various channels connected to Odoo through our connector.

Order Management made Easy across multiple E-commerce channels

  • Orders placed on various stores/marketplace can be imported to the Odoo.

  • The order status gets automatically updated on corresponding store/channel once updated in Odoo backend and vice-versa.

  • The orders can be tracked to the channel it originated for better management and delivery.

Inventory Management

  • Real-time stock update on the stores. The Product stock gets automatically updated on the website when changed in Odoo and vice-versa.

  • An entire inventory management menu designed to fulfill your advanced inventory requirements.

  • Assign and manage warehouses and location from the backend.

  • Manage products and product stock from the same screen for your multiple E-commerce channels.

  • Workout the details of every stock operation for all the E-commerce channels effectively.

Category Management

All the products at your E-commerce Sites or Marketplaces are just one click away to get imported to Odoo and thus can be managed efficiently. Categorize your Products for better search and thus to save time by Importing the categories to Odoo on just single Click!

Customer Management

  • Maintain a consolidated customer database from your multiple e-commerce channels in single Odoo.

  • The customers can be easily imported from the store/marketplace to Odoo.

  • Utilize Odoo's entire CRM menu to manage and analyze customers interactions to build customer relationships.

  • Build your extensive customer management system, generate leads, run mail marketing, and much more from a few clicks.

  • Rub promotional campaign for various customers across multiple channels/marketplaces.

  • Any changes made in the details of the customers on the stores can be easily updated in Odoo by the click of a button.

  • The data can also be set to get automatically updated with the help of Cron.

Shipping Management

  • Manage and track delivery of the orders across various channels from a single menu.

  • The shipping methods are automatically imported into the Odoo along while importing orders from the stores.

  • Delivery operations and status can be checked from the backend to avoid delays and analyze interruptions.
